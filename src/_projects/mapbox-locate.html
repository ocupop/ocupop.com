Donkey cars, self driving pizza delivery, and lenticular billboards— Mapbox Locate was about as close as you can get to navigating into the future.

Mapbox leverages the largest, most intelligent open source location platform in the world to power many of the apps we use every day. Facebook, Snapcat, Uber, Vice, Bloomberg, and Ocupop all tap into a remarkably adaptable toolset informed by 350 million miles of live road telemetry data fed into it every day.

Working on a tight timeline and a remarkable scale, Ocupop designed the 'Locate' visual system to celebrate Mapbox's authentic position at the leading edge of location data and the passionate community that joins them there.
