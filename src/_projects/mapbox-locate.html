Mapbox is an open source mapping platform that sought out Ocupop to help establish themselves as a world class tech company with branding and event design for their inaugural conference at Pier 27 in San Francisco. Equipped with a name and preliminary branding, Mapbox was looking for repositioning of their brand to reflect the bold and cutting edge developer products and culture that they are pushing out into the world.

solution_html: >-

We created a new identity system that focused on presenting Mapbox an emerging light on the map location scene. We strategized marketing campaigns and explored the edges of design with an interactive billboard, immersive stage animations, and a sophisticated, futuristic aesthetic to signage and digital assets.
