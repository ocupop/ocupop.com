A family business that began as a artful experiment turned into a full fledged passion for product design which lead co-founders Mario and Kitty Costello to form Daughter MFG. With a name picked out- inspired by their two daughters- and a lineup of handcrafted brass and resin home products, they came to Ocupop looking for an identity to call home both off and on the web.

solution_html: >-

We crafted a sophisticated brand identity that mirrors the elegant and modern yet timeless style of the products they create. It is clear they are passionate and we wanted that dedication and attention to detail to come across in the packaging and website design as an extension of the brand.
