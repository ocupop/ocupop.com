Homes by Towne is a semi-custom home developer operating across the US. With over 30 communities spanning from Florida to Hawaii, Homes by Towne has been constructing homes for nearly 70 years. Homes by Towne approached Ocupop seeking a clean and fresh rebrand and new website that made it easy for would-be home buyers to find and build their dream home.

solution_html: >-

A company so caring about it’s customers and product needed a new identity that showed that. We developed a new brand system evolving Homes’ old identity into a new mark that showcased the communities they create though stepping stone pathways and an aerial view of community plots. A hidden T mark representing the Towne company family adds a playful swoosh carried throughout the rest of the brand system.
A new website was also built with a streamlined user experience designed to compare models and drive buyers into a Homes by Towne sales office. Image styles were developed to accentuate and reflect the beauty and scenery these communities have.
