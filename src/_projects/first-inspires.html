The FIRST Inspires program creates competitions and events (both locally and internationally) to get children involved in STEM concepts as early as possible. In addition to hard skills, FIRST also teaches kids gracious professionalism and coopetition while installing their core values-- Discovery, innovation, impact, inclusion, teamwork, and fun. In combination, FIRST is setting up today’s students to be tomorrow’s innovators.
