Heirloom Liqueurs was started by Bittercube’s Proprietors, Ira and Nick, and their R&D lead, Brandon Reyes. With their collective experience in the craft cocktail industry and extensive batches of their liqueurs for their own classes and training programs, they decided it was time to bring their products to market. With Heirloom, the guys set out to create fresh twists on revered flavors, for those with an appreciation of craft/ complexity, and a desire for something new. They approached us with a need to create a brand identity, system for label design, and a website. 

solution_html: >-

We set out to create an identity that would be at once unique to Heirloom, while having a subtle, familial connection to the Bittercube brand. The H monogram is a modern take on antique, decorative letterforms, which also includes organic, leaf-like shape, symbolic of Heirloom's usage of all natural botanicals.
