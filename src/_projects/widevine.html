Widevine was founded in 1999, and acquired by Google in 2010. Widevine provides DRM solutions for the delivery of digital entertainment to any device; has made on demand services more efficient and secure for media companies, and ultimately more available and convenient for users. Widevine’s DRM solution is the industry-standard for enabling service providers to secure high quality video and audio. 

solution_html: >-

Widevine approached us They have a great product for digital rights management, but their brand fell flat in terms of being perceived as trustworthy and precision. The existing brand and website were dated, and Ocupop was brought in to reimagine the identity, restructure the information architecture of the website, and redesign the site to align with the updated identity.
