FIRST Inspires aims to get children ages 6-18 involved in STEM and STEM related fields. Each year FIRST holds a themed tournament comprising of four different competitions children participate in. Each competition presents the children with a new challenge that requires teamwork, communication, and STEM principles to complete. Oh and robots too.

solution_html: >-

FIRST came to Ocupop looking for a cohesive brand identity for their 2019 competition that felt professional while appealing to kids. Under the overarching brand, all four competitions also need identities that were tied to the theme but distinctly different. Teaser animations and illustrations were developed to spark interest alongside an overarching website explaining and informing this years theme and challenges. Clothing items and posters were also developed for participants to show their pride alongside other various swag items
