Amper is an AI music composer, performer, and producer that empowers media creators, artists, and musicians to effortlessly craft and customize original music instantly, without needing any prior music experience. Amper contacted Ocupop seeking to create a creative, clever brand identity and compelling website.

We sought to create an identity and website that reflected the creativity and technological wonder that Amper brings to the music creation community. The subtle movement and vibrant color gradients used in the website design bring the energy of the multitudes of types of music you can create.
