Google Developer Days is a multi-location web developer conference which showcases the  newest developer technologies from the top minds at Google and hosts developers from across the globe to be inspired by exciting technical talks and hands-on learning experiences. They approached Ocupop to design a cohesive brand that could be applied to 3 distinct locations and celebrates the idea of building a better web together.

solution_html: >-

Inspired by building blocks, Ocupop designed a identity system that had extensibility to be applied to the diversity in countries where this event is taking place. This was achieved through bringing a creative and vibrant design to marketing, stage and environmental design, signage, digital assets, animation, and swag items.
