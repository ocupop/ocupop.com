4P brings together individuals and families who want high-quality, fresh, natural food with the farmers who are working tirelessly to grow food in their area. They collect food from a network of over 200 farms in the D.C. area and deliver weekly produce, meat or dairy bags directly to consumers.

Ocupop created a new identity system that focused on capturing the local & friendly appeal balanced with trust and impact.
