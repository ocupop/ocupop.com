Privacy Policies

Cookies

When you visit this site, cookies will be placed on your machine for tracking visitor activity anonymously and remembering user preferences.

We set a cookie to remember that you don't want to see the pop-up at the bottom of the page that warns you about cookies on this site. If you click the "That's Cool" button, you will not see this banner on future visits* and you will opt in to having a cookie placed on your device.

This site makes use of some third party services who also store cookies (Third party cookies) on your machine. These services are

  • Google Analytics

You can click on each of the services to learn how to opt out of accepting cookies from them.

If you would like more general information on controlling cookies, please visit www.aboutcookies.org.

*Note: cookies are browser-specific. If you visit using a different browser or on another machine, you may see the banner again.

Personal Information

Ocupop only collects personal information from you when you use the contact form. This information consists of the name and email address you provide, and your IP address. We do not publish any information. We do not share any of the information collected with third parties. We only use this information to facilitate communication, specifically the ability to respond to your inquiry.
