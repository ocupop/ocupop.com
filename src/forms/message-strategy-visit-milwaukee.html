Message Strategy Questionnaire

The Message Strategy exercise is the critical first step of any successful communications campaign or project. The end results of this process establish a road map for brands to follow in campaigns and efforts across all stakeholders and media. All materials generated should speak to the principles laid out in the Message Strategy.

This preliminary process often is part of a greater holistic effort to establish a clear, consistent strategy on behalf of the brand. Whether we are merely gaining insight to better inform our work, developing a brand framework centered on brand vision and goals, or outlining and articulating a communication approach in digital or physical spaces, this effort is key in helping us—and your core audiences—understand the core of what makes your brand/product/company tick.
