Message Strategy Questionnaire

The Message Strategy exercise is the critical first step of any successful marketing or advertising campaign. The results of this process establish a road map for the company to follow in campaigns across all media. All advertising and marketing generated around a specific brand or product should speak to the principles laid out in the Message Strategy. This preliminary process often is part of a greater holistic marketing effort aiming to establish a Positioning Statement. Just as the Message Strategy establishes an advertising road map, the Positioning Statement provides the company with a distilled Brand Directive which guides actions across all aspects of business....From operations, to sales, to marketing, to shipping, to customer service, the Positioning Statement is a mantra that drills to the core of what makes your brand tick.
